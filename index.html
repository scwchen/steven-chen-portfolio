I'm Steven Chen

I am a Front-End Web Developer and Salesforce Consultant based in Toronto, ON and am a recent graduate of Juno College's Immersive Web Development Bootcamp.

Before my shift to Web Development and Consulting, I worked several years in technical support. I learned a lot from the industry but on my career change journey I fell in love with code. Read more about it here!

I pride myself on creating beautiful, responsive, and accessible content, my insatiable curiosity, and a lifelong desire to learn.
